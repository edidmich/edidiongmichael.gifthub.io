I am interested in creative expression and learning visual media and digital communication. My work explores how images and design can communicate to viewers are connect with them clearly and emotionally. I am especially interested in how digital tools and different methods are used in our everyday life. In my process I want to focus on creativity and experimentation. I enjoy working with images because they allow me to express ideas that can be difficult to explain with words alone. Visual elements such as composition, color, and texture guide attention and shape how a message can be heard Through this process, I aim to create work that feels approachable, thoughtful, and personal. As I continue learning web development and graphic communication, I want to improve my technical skills while also strengthening my creative voice. I am interested in combining design and storytelling in ways that are accessible and engaging. This course gives me the opportunity to experiment with new tools and build confidence in presenting my work digitally.
